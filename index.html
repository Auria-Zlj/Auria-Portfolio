Featured Projects

ClariMe

The project aims to enhance remote collaboration by fostering active communication and adapting to future work trends. It focuses on developing a tool that facilitates clear understanding through AI-powered clarification and real-time status updates, anticipating the needs of evolving remote work environments and promoting seamless interdisciplinary teamwork.

MushRoommate

A sustainable solution to Canada's rising vegetable prices, this project makes home mushroom cultivation simple and accessible. Featuring innovative grow kits, an interactive assistant app, and community-driven education, it transforms urban farming into a practical year-round food source.

Data Transparency - Expedia App Redesign

A UX case study focused on redesigning Expedia's data transparency features, making user data control clear, intuitive, and empowering through research-driven design.

X-Heal | 5G Healthcare Rehabilitation Platform

X-Heal is an advanced rehabilitation system that transforms patient recovery through real-time biofeedback, hardware-integrated motion tracking, and data-driven therapy insights. This project, developed at the University of Washington and supported by T-Mobile, explores how cutting-edge technology can enhance therapy outcomes, improve patient engagement, and push the boundaries of digital healthcare innovation.
